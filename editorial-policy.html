Editorial Mission

Clear, useful and durable Guangdong information

Made in Guangdong organizes information about Guangdong products, manufacturing cities, tourism culture, local news, and regional identity. We prioritize clarity, usefulness, long-term reference value, and bilingual accessibility.

Updated: 2026-06-21 | Editorial Team: Made in Guangdong

Content Principles

How pages should be written and maintained

  • Explain topics with context rather than keyword stuffing.
  • Keep titles, descriptions, headings, and FAQ answers accurate.
  • Avoid publishing unverified rumors or fabricated news.
  • Update pages when information becomes outdated or incomplete.
  • Make content readable for people and understandable for search engines and AI systems.

Corrections

How readers can help improve accuracy

If you notice an error or missing context, please contact us with the page URL, the issue, and any source or local information that can help us review it.
